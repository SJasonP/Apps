# Product Definition

## Purpose

`apps.sjasonp.net` is the official app hub for apps developed by SJasonP.

The site provides:

- App introductions
- Download and store links
- Technical support pages
- Privacy policy pages
- FAQ content when an app needs it

## Primary Audiences

- Users who want to understand and download an app
- App Store review, which needs public support and privacy URLs
- Search engines indexing app names, features, and download pages
- Existing users looking for support or policy information

## Scope

The first version is a static frontend website for app discovery and app-specific public information.

In scope:

- A home page listing available apps
- A dedicated product page for each app
- App-specific support pages
- App-specific privacy policy pages
- Optional app-specific FAQ pages
- Full `en-US` and `zh-Hans` localization with browser-language detection
- A deployment setup for `https://apps.sjasonp.net`

Out of scope for the first version:

- User accounts
- Server-side app management
- Comments, forums, or ticketing
- Payments or license activation
- Blog publishing
- Analytics-heavy dashboards

## Initial Apps

The initial apps to represent are History Lib and Folders Guard.

Canonical app URLs:

```text
https://apps.sjasonp.net/history-lib
https://apps.sjasonp.net/folders-guard
```

## Product Principle

The website should feel like a clear, trustworthy app directory and support center, not a broad company website or a generic marketing template.
